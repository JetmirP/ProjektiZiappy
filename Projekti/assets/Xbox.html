Most Popular Xbox Games

Halo 2

Halo 2 is a first-person shooter video game12developed by Bungie Studios for the Xbox video game console and is forwards-compatible with the Xbox 360.

Gears of War

The Gears of War series takes place on Sera, a fictional Earth-like planet. Human civilization develops and endures a millennia-long conflict that leaves humanity on the brink of extinction.

Forza Horizon 4

Forza Horizon 4 is a 2018 racing video game developed by Playground Games and published by Microsoft Studios.

Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic

Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic (KOTOR) is a media franchise of space western role-playing video games.

Fable

Fable games are a series of action role-playing games set in a fantasy world. The games allow the player to make choices that affect the story and the character's morality.
